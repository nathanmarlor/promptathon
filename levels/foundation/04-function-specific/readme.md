# Level 4: Function-Specific Applications

| | |
|---|---|
| **Difficulty** | ⭐⭐ Intermediate |
| **Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Tool** | Microsoft 365 Copilot |
| **Skill** | Apply AI to role-specific tasks |

---

## Objective

Apply AI prompting skills to tasks specific to your functional area, demonstrating how the CLEAR framework adapts to specialised business contexts.

---

## Business Context

General prompting skills transfer across roles, but applying AI to function-specific tasks demonstrates practical value. This level provides tailored prompts for common scenarios across different business areas.

---

## Your Task

**Step 1:** Choose the track most relevant to your role

**Step 2:** Use the sample materials or your own work content

**Step 3:** Apply the function-specific prompts

## Success Criteria

✅ Output is directly applicable to your functional context  
✅ Recommendations reflect understanding of your domain  
✅ Analysis goes beyond generic advice to specific, actionable guidance  
✅ You can use this output in real work immediately  

---

## Debrief Questions

1. How well did the AI understand your functional context?
2. What domain-specific knowledge was missing from the output?
3. How would you modify the prompt for better results next time?
