# Quick Reference Card

**Print this page** — keep it handy during exercises

---

## CLEAR Framework

| | Component | Ask Yourself |
|:--:|-----------|--------------|
| **C** | Context | What's the situation? |
| **L** | Language | What tone and style? |
| **E** | Examples | What should it look like? |
| **A** | Audience | Who is this for? |
| **R** | Request | What exactly do I want? |

---

## Template

```
Context: [situation/background]
Language: [tone/style]
Examples: [format to follow]
Audience: [who, what they know]
Request: [deliverable, length]
```

---

## Fix Problems

| Output is... | Add more... |
|--------------|-------------|
| Too generic | **Context** |
| Wrong tone | **Language** |
| Bad structure | **Examples** |
| Wrong level | **Audience** |
| Missing parts | **Request** |

---

## Iteration Phrases

> "Make it [shorter/more direct]"

> "Keep X, but change Y"

> "The tone is wrong—make it [confident/warmer]"

> "Add [element] to the [start/end]"

---

## Don't Trust AI For

- Numbers you didn't provide
- Facts you haven't verified
- Legal/compliance conclusions
- Decisions requiring your judgement

**Always verify:** Numbers, dates, names, quotes
