# Quick Reference Card

**Print this page** | Keep it handy during the hackathon

---

## CLEAR Framework

| Letter | Component | Ask Yourself |
|:------:|-----------|--------------|
| **C** | Context | What's the situation? What background does AI need? |
| **L** | Language | What tone, style, vocabulary? |
| **E** | Examples | What should it look like? Can I show a sample? |
| **A** | Audience | Who's this for? What do they know/care about? |
| **R** | Request | What exactly do I want? Format, length, elements? |

---

## Prompt Template

\`\`\`
Context: [situation/background]
Language: [tone/style/vocabulary]
Examples: [patterns to follow or reference]
Audience: [who this is for, what they know]
Request: [specific deliverable, format, length]
\`\`\`

---

## Fix Common Problems

| If output is... | Strengthen... | By adding... |
|-----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Too generic | **Context** | More situation details |
| Wrong tone | **Language** | Specific tone + example |
| Bad structure | **Examples** | Sample format to follow |
| Too complex/simple | **Audience** | Their knowledge level |
| Missing pieces | **Request** | Specific elements required |

---

## Power Phrases

**For Context:**
- "I'm preparing for..."
- "The situation is..."
- "This will be used for..."

**For Language:**
- "Tone: professional but warm"
- "Avoid jargon like..."
- "Use direct, simple language"

**For Examples:**
- "Structure like [reference]"
- "Similar to how we did [X]"
- "Follow this format: [sample]"

**For Audience:**
- "They know X but not Y"
- "They care most about..."
- "They have 2 minutes to read this"

**For Request:**
- "Maximum 100 words"
- "Create a [format] with [sections]"
- "Must include: [items]"
